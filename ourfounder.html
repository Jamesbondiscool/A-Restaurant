About The Owner

Chef James Oluwafunmilayo is the proud owner of our restaurant. With over 10 years of experience in the food industry, she has developed a passion for creating unique and delicious Nigerian dishes. Her culinary expertise has been recognized by many food critics and enthusiasts.

James Oluwafunmilayo Akingba was born and raised in Nigeria, where she learned the art of cooking from her mother. Her passion for cooking started at a young age and has only grown stronger over the years. She decided to pursue her dream of owning a restaurant to share her love for Nigerian cuisine with the world.

Her dedication to her craft has led her to constantly experiment with new flavors and techniques, resulting in a menu that is both traditional and modern. She believes in using fresh and locally-sourced ingredients to create authentic Nigerian dishes.
